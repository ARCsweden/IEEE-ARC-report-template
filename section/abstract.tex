\begin{abstract}
An Abstract is a concise summary of the whole technical report without unnecessary words.
\begin{enumerate}
    \item State the topic of the report
    \item Briefly outline your approach
    \item What you found (results)
    \item What your results mean (conclusion)
\end{enumerate}
