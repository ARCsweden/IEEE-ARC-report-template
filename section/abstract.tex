\begin{abstract}
An Abstract is a concise summary of the whole technical report, including results and conclusion without unnecessary words, recommended to write this part last\cite{techReport}. 
\begin{enumerate}
    \item State the topic of the report
    \item Briefly outline your approach
    \item What you found (results)
    \item What your results mean (conclusion)
\end{enumerate}
\end{abstract}
